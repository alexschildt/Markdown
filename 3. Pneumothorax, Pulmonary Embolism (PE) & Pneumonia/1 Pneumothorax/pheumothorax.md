# Types of Pneumothorax (PTX)
* Idiopathic/spontaneous
* Iatrogenic
* Traumatic
* Tension

# Signs and symptoms of PTX
* Chest pain
* Dyspnea
* Hypotension
* Mediastinal shift-Tension PTX

# Diagnostics
* Chest X-ray
* CT scan

# Management
* <20% - no treatment
* Needle aspiration - symptomatic-uncomplicated
* Chest tube -underlying disease
* Thorascopic exam
* Thoracotomy
* Pleurodesis - chemicals
